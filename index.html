Planets

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun. It has one natural satellite, the Moon.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It has two moons: Phobos and Deimos.

Satellites

The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite.

Phobos

Phobos is the larger and closer of Mars' two natural satellites.
